About Us

We are a group of students standing united against the EdPression we face. We convene at the Cafetaria— AKA the SELP Hall. We accept students, faculty, and non-teaching staff alike. We have a strength of over twenty members, with at least five active members at all times.

SELP has been active since 28th June, 2023.

Our Rules
